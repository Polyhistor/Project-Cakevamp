History


Before Pagevamp we ran a traditional web design agency, where we learned a lot. We realized that most people don't know how to create a website themselves and hiring us to make it for them wasn't the best solution for two reasons:

1. It isn't cheap

2. It needs maintenance


Charging people money for a site that they didn't know how to update they sometimes complain, but there weren't any other options, so we decided to make one.

And such was the birth of Pagevamp...
